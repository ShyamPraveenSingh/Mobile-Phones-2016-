TOP 10 SMARTPHONES




Apple iPhone 7, 7 Plus


Apple launched its latest flagshipsmartphones -- iPhone 7 and 7 Plus -- in India recently. The 32GB, 128GB and 256GB variants of the iPhone 7 are priced at Rs 60,000, Rs 70,000 and Rs 80,000 respectively in India. The bigger-screen iPhone 7 Plus models are priced at Rs 72,000 for 32GB, Rs 82,000 for 128GB, and Rs 92,000 for 256GB. The iPhone 7 features a single 12MP rear camera, iPhone 7 Plus comes with a dual-12MP rear camera with 2x optical zooming capability up to 56mm. Both sport a 7MP FaceTime-enabled front camera for selfies and video chats. The two iPhone 7 smartphones are powered by the new quad-core Apple A10 Fusion processor. The processor has two high performance cores and two high-efficiency cores for a longer battery life. It also has a new 6-core GPU which is 50% faster than the Apple A9 processor.

Google Pixel, Pixel XL

The month also saw the launch of first-ever made-by- Googlesmartphones, Pixel and Pixel XL. Google Pixel smartphones come in 32GB and 128GB storage options. Pixel is priced at Rs 57,000 for the 32GB version and Rs 66,000 for 128GB variant. While Pixel XL sports a price tag of Rs 67,000 for the 32GB variant and Rs 76,000 for the 128GB model. Google Pixel XL features a 5.5-inch AMOLED display with Gorilla Glass 4 protection. It also has an integrated Nexus Imprint fingerprint sensor at the back. It is powered by a quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 821 processor with 4GB RAM and comes in two storage variants - 32GB and 128GB. Pixel XL has an 8MP front-facing and a 12.3MP rear. It also includes features like Smartburst and Lens Blur. As for Pixel, the handset features a 5-inch full-HD (1080x1920 pixels) resolution AMOLED display and is backed by a 2770mAh battery with fast-charging technology. Rest of the specifications remain the same as Pixel XL.


Motorola Moto Z

Motorola launched its first modular smartphone in India. Priced at Rs 39,999, the smartphone is claimed to be the slimmest premium phonein the world at 5.2mm. Moto Z runs Android 6.0.1 Marshmallow operating system out-of-the-box and features a 5.5-inch Quad HD display. It packs a powerful Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor clubbed with 4GB RAM and 64GB internal storage. It houses a 13MP rear camera with laser-assisted autofocus, 5MP front-facing shooter and 2,600 mAh battery.

Huawei P9

Chinese smartphone maker Huaweilaunched its P9 flagshipsmartphone in India this year. Bearing a price tag of Rs 39,999, the USP of the Huawei P9 is its 12MP dual-camera setup with Leica SUMMARIT lenses. The camera setup claims to help in delivering exceptional image quality in low-light conditions. There's also an 8MP selfie shooter. On the front, there's an 8MP camera. It is powered by the company's own HiSilicon Kirin 955 octa-core processor and runs Android 6.0 Marshmallow. Sporting a 5.2-inch Full HD display, the smartphone is backed by a 3,000mAh battery.

OnePlus 3

OnePlus launched its latest flagship OnePlus 3 in India this year and it garnered accolades for its powerful performance. The smartphone sports the same price tag of Rs 27,999 as the regular version. There's also no change in specifications.OnePlus 3 runs on quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 820 processor clubbed with a massive 6GB RAM and features 5.5-inch Optic AMOLED Full-HD display with Corning Gorilla Glass on top. The phone has 16MP rear camera and 8MP front camera. It is backed by a 3000mAh non-removable battery with support for Dash Charge fast-charging technology.
